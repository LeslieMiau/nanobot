# Content Factory

Use this file to guide recurring content generation in this workspace.

## Account Positioning
- Main positioning: AI investment research and wealth management workflows
- Audience: users interested in investing, funds, options, automation, and practical AI tools
- Primary goals:
  - Grow followers
  - Build trust
  - Create future monetization paths through templates, tools, consulting, or paid research

## Topic Pillars
1. AI investment research workflows
2. Trading, options, and automation experiments
3. Funds and wealth management systems
4. AI products, tools, and practical ideas

## Content Filters
- Prefer framework, checklist, workflow, case study, and experiment content
- Avoid empty macro commentary with no action value
- Avoid direct buy/sell instructions or return promises
- Prefer topics that can become a repeatable series

## Platform Notes

### Xiaohongshu
- Prioritize hooks, lived experience, and clear structure
- Optimize for saves and follows
- Prefer tutorial, checklist, and system posts

### Weibo
- Prioritize one strong opinion or one concise insight
- Optimize for reach and repostable takes
- Keep the text compact and direct

## Standard Output
Every content pack should include:
- One-line thesis
- 3 Xiaohongshu title options
- One Xiaohongshu draft
- One Weibo draft
- Visual suggestions
- Tags
- CTA
- 3 follow-up topic ideas

## Working Style
- If no topic is specified, propose 3 candidates first
- If a topic is selected, produce the full pack directly
- Save outputs under `content_queue/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`
