# Platform Adaptation

## Xiaohongshu
- Image-first, text-second
- One visual joke or persona per card
- First card must be scroll-stopping
- Strong fit for self-recognition, comment bait, and "this is literally me" humor
- Avoid full lectures; use small scenes to imply bigger truths

## Weibo
- Short opinion + image group
- Stronger on labels and punchlines
- Better for 4-card or 6-card grouped repostable content
- End with a recognition question or lightweight provocation

## Douyin (future)
- Adapt each card into a short spoken beat
- Stronger contrast and faster setup/payoff
- Script should be one role per clip or a stitched persona montage

## X (future)
- Convert cards into concise threads or meme-post hybrids
- English or bilingual adaptation may be needed
- Tone should remain sharp, not tutorial-heavy
