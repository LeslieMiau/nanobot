# TradingCat

Default mascot for image-card generation in this skill.

## Identity
- Orange tabby cat
- Round face
- White paws
- Short tail
- Expressive eyes
- Slightly smug but fragile energy

## Tone
- Internet-friend roast
- Funny first, instructive second
- Specific situations over abstract advice

## Core Prompt Snippet
Always keep this character visually consistent:

```text
TradingCat, an original orange tabby cat character, round face, expressive eyes, slightly chubby cheeks, small triangle nose, short tail, white paws, same consistent character design
```

## Costume Rules
- Change clothes and props to express the role
- Keep the face and fur pattern stable
- Prefer finance meme props: phone portfolio, buy button, sell button, candlestick hill, briefcase, thermos, sticky notes

## Expression Library
- Overconfident
- Fomo excitement
- Tearing up but pretending calm
- Instant relief after escape
- Totally broken but still嘴硬

## Layout Rule
- One card, one role, one joke
- Keep the picture readable without the long caption
