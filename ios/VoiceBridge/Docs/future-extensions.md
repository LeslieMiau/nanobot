# Future Extensions

## Future ingress adapters

The bridge contract should be reusable for:

- `HomePod`
- `小爱同学`
- `天猫精灵`
- phones
- speakers
- car head units

## Future backends

The bridge should be backend-neutral enough to support:

- `nanobot`
- `openclaw`
- additional assistant services later

## Migration intent

The long-term goal is to move `ios/VoiceBridge/` into a dedicated repository without changing the normalized request/response contract.
